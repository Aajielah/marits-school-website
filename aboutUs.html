Our Story

MARITS Model College was founded to provide quality education to children in Keffi and beyond. We are located in Keffi, Nasarawa State.

Our goal is to nurture young minds and talents. We believe every child has the right to a sound education that prepares them for a bright future.

Our Mission

To provide quality and affordable education that builds character and excellence

Our Vision

To be a leading school in Nasarawa state, producing future leaders
